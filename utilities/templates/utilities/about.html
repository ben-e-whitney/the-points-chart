The Points Chart is a website used to manage the chore system of a co-operative house. It was written for the Dudley Co-op and is currently in alpha. If you have any questions or comments or would like an account, please send an email to admin@thepointschart.com.

The Points Chart is hosted on OpenShift and written with Django. The code, released under GPLv3, is available on GitHub. Contributions are welcome.
