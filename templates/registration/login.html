The Points Chart is a website used to manage the chore systems of co-operative houses. It was written for the Dudley Co-op and is currently in alpha. If you have any questions or comments or would like an account, please send an email to admin@thepointschart.com.

The Points Chart is hosted on OpenShift and written with Django. The site uses icons designed by Freepik.com and Dave Gandy, as well as a loading GIF generated by Preloaders.net. The code, released under the GPLv3, is available on GitHub. Contributions are welcome.
